# 🎯 Release V1.21b: Improved AI Response Rate & Status Display!

This update focuses on **AI response quality** and **real-time feedback during operation**.
Optimized thinking mode parameters reduce missed responses, and improved status display makes it easy to see what the AI is doing at a glance.
Prompt adjustments also help the AI take the conversational lead more naturally.

---

## ✨ Improvements & Fixes

### 🧠 Optimized AI Parameters for Better Response Rate

The default values for thinking mode-related parameters have been tuned.

* **temperature**: 0.7 → **1.0**
* **top_p**: 0.8 → **0.95**
* **thinking_budget**: 2048 → **1024**

Reducing the thinking budget prevents the AI from over-thinking and suppresses "empty turn" occurrences where no response is returned.
Raising temperature and top_p broadens the AI's expressive range for more natural replies.

---

### ⏱️ Improved "Thinking" Status Display

Previously, when the AI was in the "thinking" state, there was no way to tell when it would finish.

* The status now shows a **countdown** of the remaining seconds, giving you a clear sense of the wait time
* After the timeout (10 seconds), the thinking state is automatically released and the AI is ready for the next input

---

### 💬 Prompt Improvements (Japanese / English / 한국어 / Русский)

Added instructions to all standard language prompts encouraging the AI to speak up proactively.

* The AI can now develop topics naturally without being constrained by past conversation flow
* Instead of staying quiet during pauses, the AI will take the initiative and speak on its own

---

## 💡 Tips for Streamers

**Experiencing missed responses?**
This update directly addresses that issue. Lowering the thinking budget in particular reduces the "thinking too much, saying nothing" pattern.
If the problem persists, try using the `-tb 0` option to disable thinking mode entirely.
