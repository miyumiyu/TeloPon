# 🎉 Release V1.05b: Telops Move Freely! Major Boost to Speech Recognition and AI Thinking

This update is a version focused entirely on **"freedom of telop presentation"** and **"AI intelligence and recognition accuracy"**.
Multiple telops can now flow simultaneously on screen, and you can even drag them around with the mouse.
With the AI now taking a moment to "think more carefully", the quality of responses has noticeably improved.

---

## ✨ Main New Features & Changes

### 🗂️ Multiple Normal Telops Can Now Flow Simultaneously!

Previously, "normal" telops could only display one at a time, with each new response overwriting the last.
From this version, **multiple AI responses flow onto the OBS screen at the same time without overlapping**.

Previous responses remain on screen as the next response comes in alongside them — naturally creating a variety-show-style telop effect.
The pacing of conversation will feel much livelier!

---

### 🖱️ Drag Telops Around with the Mouse! (Requires OBS Setting)

The "explanation container" (the large telop that appears at the bottom of the screen) can now be **moved by dragging, resized by mouse wheel, and hidden by long-pressing**.
You can quickly adjust on the fly to suit your stream layout or the current topic.

> #### ⚙️ How to Configure in OBS
>
> To enable mouse interaction, you need to launch "**Interaction**" in OBS.
>
> 1. In the OBS sources list, **right-click TeloPon's Browser Source**
> 2. Select **"Interact"** → an interaction window opens
> 3. You can then use mouse operations (drag, scroll wheel, long-press) in that window
>
> ※ Mouse operations are only active while the interaction window is open. If you're worried about accidental input during streaming, close the window when you're done adjusting.

---

### 🧠 The AI Responds More! Improved Response Rate (Thinking Mode Enabled by Default)

Previously, even during conversation, the AI would often respond with "**👻 Noise skipped**" and stay silent.
From this version, the AI uses "a little thinking time" before responding, and **cases where it properly receives and responds have increased**.

As a trade-off, responses may take **slightly longer** than before.
But since the frustrating "nothing comes back…" situation is greatly reduced, the sense of actual conversation should be much better!

---

### 🎙️ Improved Speech Recognition Accuracy

The audio processing (chunking) sent to the AI has been improved.
There is less **missing fast speech, word endings, and short utterances**.
Recognition accuracy should naturally be higher with the same microphone settings.

---

## 💡 One-Point Tip for Streamers

**If too many telops accumulate, OBS rendering may slow down.**
Setting the UI's "Normal display duration" to a shorter value will auto-clear telops and keep things tidy.
A guideline of **5–10 seconds** is recommended.

Also, keeping thinking mode active during long streams **can increase the likelihood of AI processing overload**.
Making a habit of doing a "⬛ Disconnect → 🔴 Start Connection" refresh every 30 minutes or so will keep things running smoothly!
