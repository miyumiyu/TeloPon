# 🎉 Release V1.10b: Reply-Style Telops Arrive! Major Boost to Conversation Tempo and Stability

This update focuses mainly on **"adding variety to on-screen presentation"** and **"dramatically improved conversation tempo and stability with the AI"**.
New "reply-style" telops perfect for responding to special comments have arrived! Additionally, the entire system was overhauled to address the phenomenon where the AI would frequently go silent (empty turns), delivering a smoother, less frustrating streaming experience.

---

## ✨ Main New Features & Changes

### 💬 New "Reply-Style" Design Added (window-reply)

A special telop design has been added to indicate AI responses (e.g., replies to stream comments).
It displays in an SNS-reply or TV-subtitle style, stacking neatly from the top of the screen.

* **Default & Cool Blue support**: Clean, easy-to-read SNS-style design.
* **Support for each theme (comic / honwaka / tsundere)**: Each theme has its own dedicated two-column layout design matching that theme's aesthetic.

---

### 🧠 AI Personality (Prompt) and Comment Handling Further Evolved!

The standard selectable AI personality settings (prompts) have been overhauled to produce even more natural and entertaining reactions.
Also, the format of comments sent from plugins like YouTube integration has been unified. The AI can now more accurately understand **"who"** said **"what"**, leading to more thoughtful replies to viewers.

---

### ⚙️ Stream Tempo & Stability Dramatically Improved (No More Going Silent!)

The root cause of the phenomenon where "the AI starts to speak but then stops thinking mid-way and goes silent" (empty turns) was thoroughly investigated and dramatically reduced.

* **Auto Turn (silence prevention) optimization**: Wait times have been adjusted to prevent the system from firing in rapid succession or the AI and streamer from constantly talking over each other.
* **Full Turn Mode improvements**: Microphone unmute timing and similar adjustments have been made for smoother back-and-forth conversation.

---

### 🛠️ Debug Feature: Now You Can Peek Inside the AI's "Moments of Hesitation"

When launching in thinking mode (`-th`), a feature has been added to automatically save the AI's internal hesitation process — "why did it say nothing (why was it an empty turn)?" — as a text file.
This is a powerful ally when you're wondering "Why isn't my custom prompt working properly…?"

---

## 💡 One-Point Tip for Streamers

**How to use the new "Reply-Style" design**
The new `window-reply` design looks great when "reacting to a specific viewer's comment" via YouTube integration or similar. Try switching to the `comic` or `honwaka` theme and see how it looks in OBS!

Also, the AI has become much more talkative and the conversational tempo is better. If you find that conversations are overlapping too much, trying the improved "Full Turn Mode (launch option `-st 5`, etc.)" from this version is also recommended.
