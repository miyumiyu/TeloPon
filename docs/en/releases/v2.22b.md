## 🎮 OBS Integration Improvements

### Replay Buffer Save Support
Say "save the replay" or "keep that moment," and TeloPon will save the OBS replay buffer.  
The replay buffer must be enabled in OBS beforehand.

### UI/UX Improvements
- Added an OBS WebSocket **connect button** with clear connection status display
- On successful connection, scene list is automatically retrieved and a capture preview is shown
- On connection failure, capture, recording, and replay buffer controls are fully grayed out

---

## 📺 Niconico Live Improvements

- Stream descriptions are now parsed as HTML and displayed as rich text with links (supports `<a>`, `<strong>`, `<i>`, `<u>`, `<s>`, `<span style>`)
- Streamer icon images are now displayed

---

## 📺 YouTube Live Plugin Improvements

- Fixed an issue where thumbnails disappeared when closing and reopening the panel

---

## 🧠 AI Improvements

- Suppressed AI "hallucinations" where it fabricated viewer comments (reacting to non-existent viewers)
- Improved speaker identification between streamer voice and viewer comments
