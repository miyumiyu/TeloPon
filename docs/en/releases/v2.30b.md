## ✨ New Features

### 🎲 TRPG GM Mode — The AI Becomes Game Master, with Viewers Joining In

Two new prompts have been added: a fantasy TRPG and a Cthulhu Mythos (CoC) TRPG. The AI takes on the role of GM/KP and conducts a **viewer-participatory session** with the streamer and the audience.

- **Fantasy TRPG prompt**: A classic scenario covering combat, negotiation, and exploration
- **Cthulhu Mythos TRPG (CoC) prompt**: SAN, insanity, the 1d100 check system, and horror direction
- **The AI rolls dice accurately**: Result telops are auto-generated (rolls, modifiers, success/failure)
- **🗳️ Viewers intervene in the story via polls**: Available across YouTube / Niconico / Twitch. At story turning points ("left door? right door?", "fight? flee?"), the viewers decide the fate
- **💬 Viewer comments seep into the story**: Comments appear in-fiction as "whispers", "tavern rumors", or "messages in dreams" — woven directly into the narrative

> 📝 **The TRPG prompts are Japanese-only for now.** They appear in the prompt dropdown only when the UI language is set to Japanese. English / Korean / Russian translations are planned for a future release.

### 🎲 Voice Dice Roll — Casual Use, Even on Regular Streams

Even outside the TRPG prompts, you can have the AI roll dice with just your voice.

- Say "**Roll 2D6+3**", "**Roll a 1D6 die**", or "**Roll a die**"
- The result telop shows the notation, individual rolls, and total (e.g., 🎲 2d6+3, rolls 4 5, total 12)
- Simple display on regular streams; DC / success-failure display in TRPG contexts — switched automatically

### 💾 Story Journal — The Story Doesn't Disappear

The AI persists the "current state" of long-form content. You can resume from where you left off across reloads, reconnects, and even days.

- The AI saves automatically when the streamer says "**save the current state**" or "**save up to here**"
- A "**Carry over from last time**" UI toggle injects the previous journal at session start
- **Per-prompt management**: Fantasy GM, CoC, regular streaming — each prompt has its own journal (no mixing)
- Not just for TRPG: works for **story game streams, serialized projects, character roleplay** — any long-form content
- Say "let's start a new story" or "reset" to archive and start fresh

### 🪪 New Window Style window-status — A Telop Frame That Doesn't Disappear

A new **persistent display window that stays on screen until told to leave**. Resending it overwrites the contents in place.

- **Interactable on the OBS browser source**: drag to move, scroll to scale, double-click to temporarily hide
- The AI clears it when the streamer says "close the status"

Example uses: TRPG status (HP/SAN/MP), first-timer counter, stream uptime, viewer count, progress toward a goal — anything you want shown at all times.

### ✨ First-Timer Counter Plugin (Extension)

A plugin that detects keywords like "first time", "hello", or "初見" in comments and displays the cumulative count in window-status (a real-world implementation of the example above).

- The same viewer is only counted once (de-duplication)
- Keywords and header text are editable from the UI
- Aggregates automatically from any comment-receiving plugin (YouTube / Twitch / Niconico, etc.)
- Persistent (count carries across streams; reset is available)

## ⬆️ How to Update

Extract the downloaded ZIP over your existing folder.  
Plugin settings, prompts, and other configurations will be preserved.

> **Want to try TRPG?**: Set the UI language to **Japanese**, then select `r_trpg_gm.txt` (fantasy) or `r_trpg_coc.txt` (Cthulhu) from the "Prompt" dropdown and start the live session.
