# 🚀 Release V2.12b: Full Telop UI/UX Overhaul & Thinking Level Settings!

> 👉 **[Download TeloPon v2.12b](https://github.com/miyumiyu/TeloPon/releases/download/v2.12b/TeloPon-212b.zip)**

This update brings a comprehensive overhaul of telop visuals and interaction, along with Gemini 3.1 thinking level configuration.

---

## ✨ Full Telop UI/UX Overhaul

### 🆕 window-reply SNS Card Design

`window-reply` telops have been redesigned to look like SNS reply cards.

* **Circular avatar icon** for the commenter (supports YouTube profile images, etc.)
* **↩ reply badge** displayed at the bottom-right of the avatar
* **Slide-in animation** on telop appearance (direction auto-detected from anchor position)
* Dark background with **glow effect** (all 4 themes supported: default / comic / honwaka / tsundere)

---

### 🆕 Smoother Animations

Drag movement, telop appearance, and disappearance animations have been fully improved.

* Appearance: **smooth scale-up from scale(0)**
* Disappearance: **smooth scale-down to scale(0)** (fixes the old behavior of disappearing abruptly at half size)
* Drag movement: **instant response with no lag or stutter**
* Queue transition: when the next item is queued, **fade-out and fade-in overlap** for a seamless switch

---

### 🆕 Explain "Next" Badge / Nameplate "×" Close Button

New buttons for easier telop control.

* **Explain frame**: shows queue count + "›" badge at the bottom-right when items are queued. Click to instantly advance to the next item
* **Nameplate frame**: always-visible "✕" close button at the top-right. Click to dismiss immediately

---

### 🆕 Explain Drag Improvements

Major improvements to `window-explain` (explanation frame) drag interaction.

* Dragged position is **maintained after OBS restarts**
* Wheel zoom scale is **also preserved**
* **Double-click to reset** position and scale
* Pushing against viewport edges **compresses the frame horizontally**, pulling back expands it (40px fixed margin)
* **Timer pauses during drag** (telop won't disappear while dragging)

👉 **[Detailed guide on controlling telops in OBS](https://github.com/miyumiyu/TeloPon/blob/main/docs/en/05_obs_control.md)**

---

## ✨ Thinking Level Settings

### 🆕 Gemini 3.1 Thinking Level Support

Added support for Gemini 3.1's new thinking feature. Configure via the "💭 Thinking Level" dropdown in the UI.

| Level | Description |
|---|---|
| **minimal** | Lowest latency (default) |
| **low** | For simple tasks |
| **medium** | For moderate complexity |
| **high** | For complex reasoning and multi-step planning |

* The legacy `thinking_budget` (token count) has been removed
* Settings are saved to `settings.json` and restored on next launch
