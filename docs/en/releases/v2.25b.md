## 🔧 Fixes & Improvements

### 🔄 Reload Stability
The "Reload" button introduced in V2.24b has been significantly stabilized.

- **Fixed "freeze" issue**: Resolved a bug where the AI would stop responding after reload (`recv_response` deadlock fix)
- **Reinforced state reset**: Internal flags like `is_thinking` and `is_ai_generating` are now reliably reset when a new session starts
- **Faster recovery from abnormal-state purge**

The behavior after pressing the Reload button is now more reliable and seamless.
